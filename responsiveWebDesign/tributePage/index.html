Vitaly Dmitriyevich "Vitalik" Buterin

The young man that created Ethereum

Time line of Vitalik Buterin's life

  • 1994 - Born in Kolomna, Rusia
  • 2000 - Emigrated to Canada.
  • 2003 - While in grade three of elementary school in Canada, Buterin was placed into a class for gifted children and was drawn to mathematics, programming, and economics.
  • 2006 - Attend The Abelard School, a private high school in Toronto
  • 2007 - He played World of Warcraft from 2007 to 2010 but decided to quit after game maker Blizzard nerfed his "beloved warlock's Siphon Life spell." "I cried myself to sleep," he wrote, "and on that day I realized what horrors centralized services can bring." It may be a bit of an exaggeration to suggest that the negative experience with WoW is what inspired Vitalik to start Ethereum. But it certainly tainted his view on centralized control.
  • 2013 - Published a white paper proposing Ethereum.
  • 2014 - He dropped out of university ,when he was awarded with a grant of $100,000 from the Thiel Fellowship, and went to work on Ethereum full-time. Launched Ethereum with Gavin Wood, Charles Hoskinson, Anthony Di Iorio and Joseph Lubin.
  • 2018 - Made the “Forbes 30 Under 30” list. Furthermore, the University of Basel’s Faculty of Business and Economics awarded him an honorary doctorate.
  • 2021 - Donations included $1.14 billion in SHIBA coins to India’s crypto covid relief fund and $336 million worth of ELON tokens to Methuselah Foundation for extending human life.
  • 2022 - On February 24, Vitalik tweeted his disapproval of the Russian decision to invade Ukraine.

"If crypto succeeds, it’s not because it empowers better people, it’s because to empowers better institutions." Vitalik Buterin
